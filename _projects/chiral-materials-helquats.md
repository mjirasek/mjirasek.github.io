This project groups earlier work on chiral organic and organometallic chromophores, including helquat-based systems with strong optical and chiroptical responses.

## Molecular Design

{% include project_figure.liquid loading="eager" path="assets/img/publication_preview/2017_jpca_buckley2017tunable.png" title="Chiral helquat chromophores" %}

The scientific theme is control: designing molecular shape, charge, conjugation, and stereochemistry to tune measurable optical properties.

## Research Direction

These projects provide useful continuity with the newer work on molecular complexity and spectroscopy, where molecular structure again has to be connected to robust experimental readouts.
