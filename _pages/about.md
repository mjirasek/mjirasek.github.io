I work on experimental and computational systems that make chemistry more autonomous: robotic execution, closed-loop optimization, spectroscopy, and quantitative measures of molecular complexity. My research sits between physical organic chemistry, molecular spectroscopy, automation, and AI-assisted experiment design.
